# ONIX – AI Image Generator

ONIX is a next-generation AI Image Generator designed to transform imagination into powerful, cinematic visuals. Built with a focus on **dark aesthetics, anime style, mythological symbolism, and futuristic design**, ONIX enables creators to generate emotionally rich and visually striking images from simple prompts.

Inspired by the fusion of **human creativity and neural intelligence**, ONIX excels at producing:
- Anime-style characters with depth and mood  
- Dark fantasy and mythological-inspired visuals (symbolic, non-literal)  
- Cinematic lighting, neon highlights, and high-contrast compositions  
- Concept art, wallpapers, profile visuals, and brand imagery  

ONIX is crafted for artists, developers, storytellers, and visionaries who want more than generic AI outputs. Every image emphasizes **atmosphere, intent, and visual storytelling**.

## Features
- Advanced prompt understanding  
- Cinematic & anime-focused image generation  
- Dark, neon, myth-inspired visual styles  
- High-detail, high-contrast outputs  
- Ideal for creative projects and branding
